{% trans 'HDM was developed and is aimed at reconciling schemas from humanitarian response agencies, as OSM was traditionally developed for mapping in nonemergency situations. Humanitarian field responders need to know how OSM tags relate to their existing data models, and how to include features that may be relevant only in disaster response, recovery, or development contexts.' %}
